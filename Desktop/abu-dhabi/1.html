Abu Dhabi, UAE

Destination information

A passport valid for a minimum of six months beyond the end date of the stay is required to enter the United Arab Emirates. UK, EU, and US citizens on tourist visits lasting up to 30 days are issued visas free of charge on arrival.

Direct flights to Abu Dhabi with Etihad and BA.

Approx. 7 hours to get there, 8.5 hours to get back.

GMT +4

While the city is sunny all year round, summer can be unbearable outside and temperatures can reach more than 45°C. In the winter months, however, between October to March, temperatures range from a pleasant 20°C to 30°C. On the rare occasions it is overcast or there is a sand storm, these usually clear up within a couple of days.
