EMEA Elite 2016

The Experian Elite scheme rewards the remarkable achievement and self-motivation of those who perform in excess of their targets, as well as contribute to remarkable business results and clients satisfaction whilst embracing the People and Leadership standard and demonstrating our core DNA traits – Connect, Protect, and Create.

The reward for success in 2016 will be a tailor-made trip to Marrakesh and will be enjoyed by both EMEA Sales Elite and EMEA Heart of Experian winners.

The website will continue to be updated throughout the year providing more information about the destination and the rules and criteria to help you become an Elite winner.
